    HOW IT WORKS

    Three steps to your next walk

    01

    Pick your time

    Choose 10, 20, 30 or 60 minutes and set your energy level. Add a photo mission for extra fun.

    02

    Allow location once

    Your GPS coordinates are used locally in your browser to calculate a loop near you. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere.

    03

    Walk, earn XP, level up

    Follow the waypoints in Google Maps. Complete the walk to earn XP, unlock achievements, and keep your daily streak.

    A WALKING IDEA FOR RIGHT NOW

    Where should I walk today?

    Random Walk removes the hardest part of going outside: choosing a direction. Select a duration and receive a new set of nearby waypoints plus an optional photo challenge — every time, for free.

    Use it for a daily walking challenge, a screen break, a spontaneous date, or a weekend adventure. No account required. No data collected. Earn XP and level up just by walking.
