WALKING CHALLENGES

21 ways to make a normal walk feel new

Choose one mission before leaving the door, or let the Random Walk Generator pick your route and photo mission automatically.

Photo challenges

  1. Photograph five objects of exactly one color.
  2. Find a reflection that surprises you.
  3. Photograph shadows that form letters or shapes.
  4. Find a door that tells a story.
  5. Spot three different types of window.
  6. Find and photograph something smaller than your hand that is beautiful.
  7. Capture a moment of color in an otherwise grey street.

Navigation challenges

  1. Take every second safe left turn for ten minutes.
  2. At each intersection, choose the quietest-looking street.
  3. Walk until you find a street you have never entered, then explore it fully.
  4. Choose turns using a coin flip — only where safe.
  5. Head toward the tallest visible building without following a direct route.
  6. Choose a landmark and approach it indirectly by a new path.

Observation challenges

  1. Find the oldest date engraved on a building.
  2. Spot three different types of tree within one block.
  3. Count dogs for fifteen minutes.
  4. Find public art smaller than a postcard.
  5. Notice five sounds you normally ignore.
  6. Find a building with an animal symbol.

Personal challenges

  1. Walk one block further than your usual turning point.
  2. End every walk with a photo of your single best discovery.
