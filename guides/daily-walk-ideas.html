DAILY WALK IDEAS

Where should I walk today?

Start with the random walking route generator for an instant suggestion, or choose one of these walk formats to suit your mood.

The 10-minute reset

Walk five minutes away from your starting point, pause for thirty seconds, and return by a different safe street. Best used as a screen break during a long work session.

The unfamiliar block

Choose one block you usually pass without entering and walk around its full perimeter. Observe details — doors, windows, plants — that you have always ignored.

The photo walk

Pick one subject — doors, typography, plants, reflections, or textures — and collect five photographs before returning home. Use the generator's photo mission for a randomly chosen subject.

The quiet route

At every safe intersection, choose the street that appears the least busy. Walk for 20 minutes and see how far from the noise you can get.

The social walk

Invite someone and take turns choosing each safe direction. No destination planned — conversation is the goal.

The challenge walk

Accept one of the app's daily challenges before leaving, then complete your walk knowing exactly what to aim for. Earn XP and maintain your streak.
