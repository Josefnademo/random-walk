ABOUT

A tiny cure for indecision.

Random Walk is a free, browser-based tool that generates spontaneous walking routes near your current location. Pick a duration, hit generate, and follow the waypoints in your favourite map app.

How it works

After you tap "Generate my walk", your browser asks for location permission once. Your GPS coordinates never leave your device — they are used purely in the browser to calculate a circular loop of waypoints around you using the Haversine formula.

Not a navigation service

The generated waypoints are approximate. The generator cannot verify pedestrian access, private property, construction zones, or local safety conditions. Always review and adjust your route in a real map application before walking, and apply common sense throughout.

Gamification & storage

Your XP, level, achievements, walk count, and streak are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. You can clear this data at any time via your browser settings.

Why static?

A pure static website is fast, free to host on GitHub Pages, easy to audit, and keeps all logic client-side — which is exactly what privacy-first apps should be.
