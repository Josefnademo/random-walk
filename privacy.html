PRIVACY

Your location stays local.

Random Walk is built as a fully static web application. There is no server, no backend, no account system, and no database. This page explains exactly what data the app handles.

Location data

After you press "Generate my walk", your browser requests your GPS location using the standard browser Geolocation API. Your coordinates are processed entirely inside your browser to calculate walking waypoints. They are never transmitted to any server, never stored in any database, and never shared with any third party.

Location access can be revoked at any time via your browser settings.

Local storage (on-device only)

The following data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage — only on your device, only readable by this website:

  • Total walks completed
  • Total kilometres walked
  • XP, level, and achievement progress
  • Daily challenge completion state
  • Current and longest walk streaks
  • App settings (units, theme)

You can clear all of this at any time via your browser's storage settings or developer tools.

External services

When you tap "Open in Google Maps" or "View in OpenStreetMap", the generated coordinates are passed to those external services via a URL. Their own privacy policies apply once you leave this site.

The Leaflet map tiles are loaded from CARTO's tile servers (OpenStreetMap data). Standard server logs may be created by their infrastructure; see CARTO's privacy policy ↗.

The optional QR code in the Invite section is generated by the free QR Server API ↗. Only the site URL is passed — no personal data.

Analytics

No analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts are included on this website.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies.

Contact
